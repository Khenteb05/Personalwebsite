About Me

Student/Blogger

Welcome to my personal haven, where stories are told via words, passions flare, and curiosity knows no limits! I'm happy to greet each one of you, fellow explorers, thinkers, and inspiration seekers. I am Karma Drupthop, 23 years old, an inquisitive individual with an abundant heart full of aspirations and a mind full of curiosity from the Land of Thunder Dragon, Bhutan. I am currently pursuing my masters in engineering practice typically focusing in smart and renewable energy in Murdoch university. I'm inviting you to come along as we examine the colourful tapestry of life together as I set out on this path of self-discovery and creative self-expression.I've always been captivated by the power of imagination and the beauty of storytelling. I've always found comfort and happiness in the creative realm, whether it was losing myself in the words of my favorited songs, savouring every word of a compelling novel, or taking pictures of moment.
